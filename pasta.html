Pasta



Pasta is a beloved Italian dish known for its comforting taste and endless variety. Whether it’s creamy Alfredo, spicy Arrabbiata, or classic tomato basil, pasta adapts to every flavor and occasion. Made from simple ingredients like flour and eggs, its charm lies in its versatility—twirled, baked, or stuffed, it’s a timeless favorite around the world.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Pasta
  • 2 cups Water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot
  2. Add pasta and cook until al dente
  3. Drain the pasta and rinse with cold water
  4. Heat olive oil in a pan
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the pan and toss to coat
  6. Season with salt to taste
  7. Serve hot and enjoy your meal!
