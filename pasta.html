Pasta

Pasta is very tasty

I just love pasta

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of pasta
  • 2 cups of water
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a pot.
  2. Add salt and pasta to the boiling water.
  3. Cook for 8-10 minutes or until al dente.
  4. Drain the pasta and add olive oil.
  5. Serve hot with your favorite sauce.
