Ingredients

  • Minced Meat
  • Onions
  • Spices
  • Bell Pepper

Steps

  1. Mix minced meat with spices and chopped onions.
  2. Shape the mixture into kebabs.
  3. Grill the kebabs until cooked through
  4. Serve with bell peppers and your favorite sauce
  5. Serve hot with raita
