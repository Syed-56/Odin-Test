Biryani

This is a photo of a delicious biryani

Biryani is my Favorite Food

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Spices
  • Veggies

Steps

  1. Wash the rice
  2. Marinate the chicken with spices
  3. Cook the rice and chicken together
  4. Add veggies and cook until done
  5. Serve hot with raita
