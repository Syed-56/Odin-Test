Biryani



Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic South Asian rice dish that combines fragrant basmati rice with tender meat, spices, and herbs. Often considered a symbol of celebration, biryani comes in many regional variations such as Hyderabadi, Sindhi, and Karachi-style, each offering its unique blend of spices and cooking techniques. The dish is typically layered, with marinated meat placed between layers of parboiled rice, then steamed together to infuse all the flavors. Ingredients like saffron, fried onions, mint, and yogurt elevate the taste, making biryani a beloved comfort food across generations. Ingredients

  • Rice

Steps

  1. Wash the rice
  2. Marinate the chicken with spices
  3. Cook the rice and chicken together
  4. Add veggies and cook until done
  5. Serve hot with raita
